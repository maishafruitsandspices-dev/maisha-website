About Maisha

We Are More Than
a Juice Company

Maisha means life in Swahili — and that name is our promise. We exist to bring vibrant, natural fruit goodness to every table, while building lasting livelihoods for Africa's smallholder farmers.

Founded in 2018, Maisha operates at the intersection of agriculture and consumer goods. Our two flagship initiatives — the Fruit Up juice brand and the Maisha Growers' Scheme — form a vertically integrated ecosystem that benefits everyone in our value chain.

Maisha Growers' Scheme

Growing Together,
Thriving Together

The Maisha Growers' Scheme is our commitment to Africa's backbone — the smallholder farmer. We provide the tools, training, and guaranteed market access that transforms smallholder farming into a sustainable enterprise.

📋

Guaranteed Off-Take Agreements

Direct contracts at fair, pre-agreed prices — no more uncertainty at harvest.

🎓

Free Agri-Training

Expert agronomists, GAP workshops, and digital farming resources at no cost.

💧

Input Support

Subsidized quality seeds, organic inputs, and irrigation support for registered growers.

📱

Digital Farmer App

Track harvests, receive payments, and access live market prices from your phone.
